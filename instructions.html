Instructions

You are a wizard stopping ogres from storming your castle!

1. You have three minutes to protect your castle from being destoyed.
2. Use your cursor to aim your spells at the ogres. each spell can only kill one ogre.
3. One point is added for each ogre killed
4. You have five lives. Lose one life for every ogre that enters the castle.
5. Don't lose all lives in three minutes to win!
